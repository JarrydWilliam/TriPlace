# SameVibe Founder Decision Lock

## 1. Group-first connections
* There is no public people-search directory.
* Viewing a profile does not automatically permit unsolicited messaging.
* First communication occurs through:
  * Joined communities
  * Group activities
  * Event-attendance groups
  * Moderated community discussions
* SameVibe will not launch with direct one-on-one matching.
* Blocking applies across relevant discovery, attendance, messaging, activity, and notification paths.
* A blocked user cannot bypass the block through another shared activity.

## 2. Adults-only launch
* DOB is collected.
* Eligibility is calculated from the submitted birth date.
* Exactly-18 users are handled correctly.
* Underage users cannot access social features.
* Apple and Google authentication cannot bypass the SameVibe age gate.
* Existing users missing compliance data remain restricted.
* Full DOB is not exposed in public responses.
* The product does not claim government-ID verification.
