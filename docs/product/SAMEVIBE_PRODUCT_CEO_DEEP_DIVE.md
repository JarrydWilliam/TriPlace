# SameVibe Product CEO and Product Director Deep Dive

## 1. Immediate activity creation
* An eligible adult can access activity creation immediately after onboarding.
* Group-oriented activity structure must be protected.
* General location must be used before participation.
* Private residential addresses are prohibited.
* Participant privacy must be protected.
* Reasonable group-size expectations must be enforced.
* Disguised one-on-one solicitation is prohibited.
* External contact details are not permitted as a bypass.
* Safety acknowledgment is required.
* Activity limits for new accounts apply.

## 2. RSVP behavior
* Removes any false or nonfunctional verification promise.
* Allows a valid adult user with baseline trust level to RSVP.
* Does not claim identity verification.
* Does not bypass a server-side safety restriction.
* Does not expose private participant information.
* Does not create duplicate registrations.
* Keeps the user within a group or event-participation context.
* Works for both SameVibe activities and imported external events where applicable.

## 3. Safety and human oversight
* Reporting remains functional.
* Blocking remains functional.
* Account deletion remains functional.
* Moderation intake remains functional.
* Severe or irreversible decisions retain human approval.
* No automated system is presented as guaranteeing user safety.
* No remaining copy falsely claims comprehensive identity verification.

## 4. Nuance: One-on-one activity partners
* Users may express interest in finding activity partners.
* The launch experience must still introduce them through communities, activities, attendance groups, or another shared group context.
* Do not implement unrestricted one-on-one discovery or unsolicited direct messaging.
* A controlled mutual connection after shared context is a future feature, not part of Release Set A.
